This Privacy Policy explains how Caivra Tech LLC handles information when you use Mimic Studio on Android devices.

Mimic Studio does not sell personal information. Mimic Studio is not designed to track you across unrelated apps or websites.

Microphone and Audio Access

Mimic Studio requests microphone access only when you choose to record audio. The app uses microphone access to record humming, vocals, and audio ideas. You can change microphone permission from your device settings at any time.

File and Media Access

If you choose to import or export audio files, Mimic Studio may request access needed to select, save, or share files. File access is used only to complete the action you choose inside the app.

How Information Is Used

  • To provide recording, importing, editing, arrangement, playback, and export features.
  • To preserve your creative session and project settings.
  • To respond to support requests and investigate reported issues.
  • To improve app stability, performance, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal, safety, or security obligations when required.

Audio Processing and Storage

Audio files and project data may be stored locally on your device as part of normal app functionality. If you choose to export, share, or send a file outside the app, that action is controlled by the destination you select.

If Mimic Studio offers an online processing feature, audio or project data you intentionally submit to that feature may be transmitted to service providers solely to complete the requested function. We do not use your audio recordings to sell personal information or to track you across unrelated services.

Sharing of Information

We may share limited information with service providers who help operate, diagnose, host, secure, or improve Mimic Studio. These providers are expected to use information only for the services they provide to us. We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or to address security issues.

Retention

Local audio files and projects remain on your device until you delete them, remove the app, or use device-level storage controls. Support emails may be retained as needed to respond to your request, maintain business records, and comply with legal obligations.

Your Choices

  • You can deny or revoke microphone permission in device settings.
  • You can delete projects, recordings, and exported files from your device where available.
  • You can contact us to request help with privacy questions or support records.

Children's Privacy

Mimic Studio is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as Mimic Studio changes. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the latest version.
